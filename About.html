About Us

Greedy Aces was born out of a shared love for Counter-Strike and a burning desire to elevate esports in India to new heights.

Our Founders:

Siddhartha Halder, known in the gaming realm as ZANE, and Adarsh Singh Sikharwar, better recognized as Euphoria, brought a wealth of experience and unmatched enthusiasm to the table. As veterans of Counter-Strike, they understood the game's intricacies like no other and aimed to leave an indelible mark on the esports landscape.

A Journey of Triumph:

Over the years, Greedy Aces has transcended expectations, consistently pushing the boundaries of what's possible in competitive gaming. Our roster of talented players has not only secured victory in multiple prestigious tournaments but has also frequently found themselves in the heart-pounding semi-finals and finals. Each match, each win, and each loss has been a stepping stone towards our ultimate goal - conquering the esports scene in India and beyond.

Our relentless pursuit of excellence, combined with an unwavering commitment to teamwork and innovation, has cemented Greedy Aces as a force to be reckoned with in the world of esports.

The Essence of Greedy Aces:

At Greedy Aces, we believe that gaming is more than just a pastime; it's a way of life. Our journey has been driven by a shared passion for the game, a burning desire to improve, and the thrill of competition. We understand that esports isn't just about victory; it's about the friendships formed, the lessons learned, and the indomitable spirit that keeps us pushing forward, no matter the odds.

Quote for Avid and Passionate Gamers:

"To truly understand the soul of a gamer, you must look beyond the screen. It's in the late-night practice sessions, the camaraderie with teammates, and the unyielding passion that sets the heart ablaze. We are the Greedy Aces, and we're here to redefine the game."

Join us on this incredible journey, as we continue to chase our dreams, break boundaries, and inspire the next generation of esports enthusiasts. Together, we'll script a legacy that will be etched in the annals of gaming history. Greedy Aces - Where Passion Meets Precision, and Victory Awaits.
